Tor Metrics Portal: Research


The Tor Metrics Project aims at supporting privacy enhancing technologies research by making papers and technical reports, gathered network data, and tools for processing these data available to the public. If you are missing anything for your Tor-related research or want to share your research results with others, please let us know!
